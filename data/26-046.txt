
They were standing on the edge of the snowy field, their feet sinking into the soft, white snow beneath them. The sky above them was a deep shade of blue, with no clouds to obscure the view. In the distance, a group of people could be seen, likely enjoying the crisp, cold air as well.

"This is so beautiful," she breathed, taking in the expanse of the snowy field. "I've never seen anything like it."

"Me neither," he replied, his eyes fixed on her. "It's like we're the only two people in the world."
