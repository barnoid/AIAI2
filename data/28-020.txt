
"Hey, how long have you been walking alone?" a voice called out to him. He turned around to see a woman standing behind him. "I've been looking for you," she added. "I'm sorry, I didn't see you there," he apologized. "It's alright," she smiled. "I'm not in a hurry to get anywhere." They started walking together, and he felt a sense of relief wash over him. He had finally met someone who was a good match for him.
