
As he walked, the boy found himself lost in thought. He wondered about his past and his future, and what life had in store for him. Suddenly, he felt someone tugging at his sleeve, and he turned to see a girl standing beside him. "Can I talk to you for a moment?" she asked. "I'm lost and looking for a way to get back home." He looked at her, and for the first time, he saw her face. "I can't help you," he replied. "But if you're lost, I can show you the way."
