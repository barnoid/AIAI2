
"Thanks for stopping by, have a great evening," the clerk, a tall, thin man with a beard, said to the family.

"You too," the mother replied as they walked out of the store.

As they stepped onto the sidewalk, the mother noticed a small puppy running around, begging for attention. She couldn't help but smile, as she picked up the puppy and held it in her arms.

"Look, we have a new friend," she said, as she turned to the father, who was looking back at the puppy with a smile on his face.
