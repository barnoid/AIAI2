
"I don't like this place," the woman spoke softly, breaking the stillness. "It's too quiet."

"It's not just us they're watching," the man replied, his voice low. "It's something in the woods."

As they paused to catch their breath, they noticed a flicker of light in the distance. The glimmer of a torch or lantern, perhaps belonging to someone they couldn't see. The mysterious light piqued their curiosity, and they pressed on, drawn towards the source of the light.
