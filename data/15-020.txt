
"I wish I could be one of them," he thinks to himself, looking around at the people in the restaurant. The smell of food and the noise of people eating fills the room, creating a warm, inviting atmosphere. He sighs, knowing that his life is not yet ready to join in with theirs.

"Why must I stand alone?" he wonders, his eyes following a waitress as she moves around the restaurant. She is a blur of motion, her actions graceful and efficient, a contrast to his own sluggish, heavy movements.

As the man watches the waitress, a young man in a black hat walks into the restaurant. The man in the suit looks up, a spark of recognition igniting in his eyes. He smiles, his face lighting up with the anticipation of new friends and new experiences.
