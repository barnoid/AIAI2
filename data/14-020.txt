"I've seen strange things before, but never anything like this. I think it's something important, and we need to figure out what it is." The man nodded, his brow furrowed with concentration. "I agree. But where do we start? We need to find out more about these objects and the light itself." The couple, determined to solve the mystery, began their investigation, unaware of the danger that awaited them.
