
As the man and woman examined the strange artifacts, they noticed a peculiar sound. "Did you hear that?" the woman whispered, her eyes wide with surprise. The man listened closely, and he too heard the faint humming noise. They looked around the room, searching for its source. It seemed to be coming from a corner, but as they approached, the noise grew louder. The man gasped as he saw a strange, shimmering light emanating from the corner of the room. The woman's eyes were wide with fear, and they both knew they were in for a dangerous journey.
